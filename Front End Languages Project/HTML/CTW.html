Mission

Code the Way facilitates the collaboration of schools, companies, and nonprofits to: (1) Offer students advanced computer science training and real-world job experiences working on projects that benefit nonprofits; (2) Increase certified computer science teachers; and (3) Contribute to the technology talent base.

Vision

High school students in Greater Milwaukee have access to high quality computer science education and real-world job experiences, positioning them for success in technology-related fields.
